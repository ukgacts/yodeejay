Biography

SuperStyleDeluxe first emerged in the late '90s as a rising DJ talent in the West Midlands. Honed his skills crafting mixtapes and rocking the decks at the legendary Stump Juice nightclub in Wolverhampton, where the name "SuperStyleDeluxe" was born and quickly stuck.

The unique, high-energy sound soon caught the attention of Hamburg's Lounge Records. Produced a succession of singles and a string of popular remixes for major labels like BMG and Sony, working with artists like Bent, Pink, and Dido. This success led to chart-topping releases and nominations for prestigious awards such as Breaks Poll. He further cemented his status with releases on influential labels like Supercharged and Against the Grain in the mid 2000s.

Never one to rest on his laurels, SuperStyleDeluxe launched The Payback Project, a pioneering label that quickly became a driving force in the scene. With a relentless pursuit of innovation and a commitment to pushing boundaries, The Payback Project helped shape the sound of a generation.

Now, after a brief hiatus, SuperStyleDeluxe is back in 2023, bringing his evolved sound to the UK Garage and Bass scene. Get ready for the next chapter.

But SuperStyleDeluxe is more than just a producer. He's a skilled turntablist known for his dynamic DJ sets that seamlessly blend technical skill with an infectious energy. His worldwide adventures have taken him from BBC Radio One the UK club circuit to tours across Australia, New Zealand, Asia, North America, Russia, and Europe, sharing the stage with acts like Pendulum. His passion for DJing even led him to collaborate with Ecler, contributing to the development of their NUO and HAK scratch mixers as part of the Ecler Pro Team.
